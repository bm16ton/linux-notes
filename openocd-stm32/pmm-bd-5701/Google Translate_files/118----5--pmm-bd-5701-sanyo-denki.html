This driver board is used for stepper motors of 5 phases, and with the help of it, stepper motors up to 2 amps can be started. Its operation is very simple.

How to set up and operate this driver

Arrange the bases

1 and 2 - input and main power supply of the stepper motor, which should not be less than 12 and more than 32 (24 volts is recommended)

3 and 4- GND main power supply driver

5- 5 volt power supply to start the driver's internal circuit (this base does not consume much current and can be connected to the 5 volt power supply of the microcontroller)

6- Connect the micro microcontroller

7- Pulse input for clockwise direction

8- Pulse input for anti-clockwise direction

9- Turn the drive on and off

10- Half step activation base (doubles stepper accuracy)

11- Driver output pulse for feedback (as open collector)

12- It has no application

Pulse and direction conversion map to CW, CCW:

With this circuit, you can easily use this driver with pulse input and direction.

Most stepper motor control modules have pulse / direction output. This means that the pulse is sent on one line and the direction command on the other line, but in this driver the command method is different and it has two pulse inputs that will be applied to each pulse and will rotate in the same direction. For ease of operation, the pulse / direction converter circuit to the CW / CCW driver is located at the bottom.
